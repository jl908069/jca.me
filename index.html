Ph.D. student, Department of Linguistics, Georgetown University · yl1290 [at] georgetown [dot] edu

My name is Jessica (Yi-Ju) Lin (林宜儒). I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Linguistics at Georgetown University, where I’m advised by Amir Zeldes and a member of Corpling Lab and Computational Linguistics @ Georgetown (GUCL). Before coming to Georgetown, I recieved my M.A. in Linguistics from National Taiwan University and B.A. in Applied Chinese Language and Culture from National Taiwan Normal University.

Research Interests:

  • Hate Speech Detection, Multimodal Hate Speech Detection
  • Entity Linking, Salient Entity Detection
  • Corpus Linguistics
  • Societal applications of NLP, NLP for Social Good
